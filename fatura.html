description

Its a simple repository management with invoice support. Designed for small enterprices that need to keep their numbers under control. It allows product registration with their quantities (ammong other details) and invoice bill calculation, storage and printing.



features

Fatura supports initial poduct registration, in bulk or individualy



Registration of qualities (product qualities to diferentiate between similar products) that can be applied separately from the product they were registered

Search for the desired product among all the registered products

Automatic calculation of remaining of inventory by calculating incoming supply and individual sales

Chosing a existing product to modify and store it as a different one



Security features regarding filling forms and finalizing decisions



Display of chosen product with their prices calulated automatically based on the quantity. Also allows the calculation of total vlue based on tax value and special offers(lowering the price)

It supports open account regarding the invoice bill. For clients that have long term projects and need supplies through out time, they don't have to pay every time their account stays open and its value is added up every time.
